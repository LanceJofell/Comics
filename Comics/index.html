Wind Breaker

"Wind Breaker" is a webtoon series following Jay, a high school student who discovers a passion for competitive road cycling. The series explores Jay's journey as he faces challenges, forms friendships, and competes in thrilling races. It delves into the demanding world of road cycling, emphasizing perseverance, teamwork, and self-discovery. With dynamic art depicting intense racing scenes, "Wind Breaker" is a compelling story of personal growth and determination in the world of cycling.
